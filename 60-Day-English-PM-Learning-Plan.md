# 60-Day English + PM/Startup Learning Plan

> Using Lenny's Podcast Transcripts to improve English while mastering product management and startup skills

---

## How to Use This Plan

### Daily Structure (45-60 minutes)

| Activity | Time | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| **Read & Listen** | 20 min | Read transcript section while listening (if audio available) |
| **Vocabulary** | 10 min | Extract 5-10 new words/phrases, write definitions |
| **Shadowing** | 10 min | Read aloud, mimicking the speaker's rhythm and intonation |
| **Summary Writing** | 10 min | Write a 3-5 sentence summary in your own words |
| **Reflection** | 5 min | Record yourself explaining one key insight |

### Weekly Review (Weekends)
- Review all vocabulary from the week
- Write a 200-word reflection combining insights from that week
- Practice explaining concepts to an imaginary colleague

## Vocabulary Master List by Category

### Strategy & Business
| Word | Definition | Example Sentence |
|------|------------|------------------|
| delight | exceed expectations | "Our goal is to delight customers" |
| margin | profit per unit | "We need margin-enhancing features" |
| hard-to-copy | defensible, moat | "Brand trust is hard-to-copy" |
| flywheel | self-reinforcing cycle | "The flywheel is spinning faster" |
| moat | competitive advantage | "Network effects create a moat" |

### Growth & Metrics
| Word | Definition | Example Sentence |
|------|------------|------------------|
| ARR | Annual Recurring Revenue | "We hit $1M ARR" |
| retention | users who stay | "90% monthly retention" |
| activation | first value moment | "Activation happens at first match" |
| cohort | group of users | "The January cohort retained well" |
| churn | users who leave | "We reduced churn by 20%" |

### Leadership & Career
| Word | Definition | Example Sentence |
|------|------------|------------------|
| scope | breadth of responsibility | "Increase your scope" |

| delegation | giving work to others | "Delegation is essential" |
| executive presence | leadership aura | "Develop executive presence" |
| sponsor | career advocate | "Find a sponsor, not just mentor" |

### Product & Design
| Word | Definition | Example Sentence |
|------|------------|------------------|
